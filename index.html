Billions spent on passive cameras that watch incidents happen and do nothing without a human watching.

Traditional surveillance is passive. Cameras record — they do not act. 911 depends on bystanders to notice, dial, and describe. Fires spread. Shots ring out. Riots escalate. Officers respond blind. Sentinel Network changes the equation: AI detects the incident, determines the location, and alerts the right unit before a bystander even unlocks their phone.

Operational Flow

From Detection to
Dispatch in Seconds

📷01
AI Detects an Incident

A Sentinel drone or fixed camera captures live footage. YOLOv8 scans every frame for fights, fires, weapons, riots, shootings, and suspicious activity — 24/7.

🧠02
AI Classifies & Locates

The model identifies event type and confidence level. GPS metadata tags the exact location — down to the block — in real time.

🔔03
Alert Fires Instantly

The live dashboard updates and an SMS alert fires to dispatch within seconds — event type, clip, location, and confidence score included.

🚘04
Nearest Unit Dispatched

The dispatcher routes the nearest available unit — police, fire, or EMS depending on the incident type. No civilian call needed.

What Sentinel Detects

Every Threat. Every Camera.
Every Second.

Live Now 👊
Physical Altercations

Real-time detection of fights, brawls, and physical assaults using pose estimation and motion analysis. Currently live and running in production on active camera feeds.

Phase 3 🔥
Fire & Smoke

Thermal signature and visual smoke detection for structure fires, vehicle fires, and arson events — alerting fire departments before 911 calls come in.

Phase 3 🔫
Weapon Detection

Computer vision identification of firearms, knives, and other weapons in public spaces. Flags armed individuals before an incident escalates.

Phase 3 🚨
Riots & Crowd Escalation

Crowd density analysis and behavior pattern detection to identify gatherings that are escalating into riots, looting, or civil unrest before they become unmanageable.

Phase 3 💥
Gunshot & Shooting Events

Visual flash detection and scene-context analysis to identify active shooter situations and gunfire events, dispatching armed units with immediate GPS precision.

Phase 3 🚗
Suspicious Vehicles

License plate recognition and behavioral pattern analysis to flag stolen vehicles, abandoned packages, and vehicles matching active crime alerts in real time.
